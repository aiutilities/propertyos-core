# ADR-004: NestJS Backend

## Status

Proposed

## Context

PropertyOS needs a structured backend framework suitable for APIs, modules, permissions, events, plugins, and long-term maintainability.

## Decision

NestJS will be the preferred backend framework.

## Consequences

- Modular backend structure.
- Good fit for TypeScript teams and AI-assisted development.
- Requires discipline to avoid over-engineering early.
