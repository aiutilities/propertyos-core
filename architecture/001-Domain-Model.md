# PropertyOS Domain Model

## Purpose

This document defines the core business concepts of PropertyOS.

These concepts may later become modules, APIs, workflows, plugins, events, or database entities.

---

## Organization

An entity operating one or more properties.

Examples:

- Apartment Association
- Property Management Company
- Coworking Operator
- Educational Institution
- Industrial Park Operator

---

## Property

A physical location managed within PropertyOS.

Examples:

- Residential Community
- Commercial Complex
- Campus
- Coworking Facility
- Industrial Estate

---

## Building

A physical structure within a property.

A property may contain one or more buildings.

---

## Zone

A logical or physical area within a property.

Examples:

- Tower A
- Reception Area
- Parking Area
- Security Gate
- Common Area

---

## Space

A manageable unit within a property.

Examples:

- Apartment
- Office
- Room
- Meeting Room
- Shop
- Desk
- Parking Slot

---

## Person

A human known to the platform.

Examples:

- Resident
- Visitor
- Staff
- Vendor
- Owner
- Tenant

---

## Organization Member

A person associated with an organization.

Examples:

- Admin
- Manager
- Security Staff
- Maintenance Staff

---

## Visitor

A person temporarily entering a property.

---

## Vendor

An external service provider.

Examples:

- Electrician
- Plumber
- Housekeeping Company
- Internet Provider

---

## Asset

A physical or digital resource.

Examples:

- CCTV Camera
- Generator
- Lift
- Vehicle
- Equipment

---

## Document

A stored record.

Examples:

- Agreement
- Invoice
- ID Proof
- Visitor Pass
- Policy Document

---

## Workflow

A repeatable business process.

Examples:

- Visitor Approval
- Asset Maintenance
- Complaint Resolution
- Vendor Approval

---

## Event

A significant action that occurred in the system.

Examples:

- Visitor Invited
- Visitor Checked In
- Asset Created
- Workflow Approved

---

## Notification

A communication generated by the platform.

Channels:

- WhatsApp
- Email
- SMS
- Push Notification

---

## Plugin

An installable business capability.

Examples:

- Visitor Management
- Asset Management
- Facility Management
- Helpdesk

---

## Theme

A presentation layer that controls user experience and branding.

---

## Distribution

A packaged solution built on PropertyOS Core.

Examples:

- CommunityOS
- CommercialOS
- CoworkOS
