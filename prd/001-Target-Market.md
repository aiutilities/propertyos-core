# PropertyOS Target Market

## Who is PropertyOS for?

PropertyOS is for organizations that manage physical properties, communities, and shared spaces.

## Primary Target Users

- Residential communities
- Commercial complexes
- Coworking spaces
- Educational campuses
- Industrial parks
- Managed facilities

## Positioning

PropertyOS is not an apartment management system.

PropertyOS is an open-source platform upon which apartment management systems, visitor management systems, coworking management systems, facility management systems, and custom property solutions can be built.

## Ideal Early Users

Early users should be operators who need:

- Data ownership
- Plugin-driven extensibility
- API-first architecture
- Workflow automation
- WhatsApp-first communication
- Self-hosted or cloud-hosted deployment

## Not For Now

PropertyOS is not initially focused on:

- Full ERP replacement
- Accounting software
- CRM software
- Single-purpose apartment software
- Marketplace-first business model
